Frequently Asked Questions

Do you detail in Cinco Ranch and Seven Meadows?

Yes — Cinco Ranch and Seven Meadows are two of our most common Katy neighborhoods. We work in driveways, garages, and community parking areas. Most Katy HOAs allow professional mobile detailing services on private property.

Can you do ceramic coating in my Katy garage?

Yes. Ceramic coating requires a covered, dust-controlled environment — a garage is ideal. We bring our own lighting and all application materials. If you have a standard two-car garage, that's all we need.

How often should I detail my car in Katy?

We recommend a full exterior detail every 3–4 months for cars parked outside in Katy's sun. Interior every 4–6 months depending on use. If your car has ceramic coating, a maintenance wash every 6 weeks keeps the coating performing at peak level.
